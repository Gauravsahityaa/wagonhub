About Us

Go Wagon Co. Is a Kuwaiti Logistics Company founded in late 2017 and developed to an on-demand logistic application in 2019.Since the start, we have focused on services quality, speed and safe delivery. Go Wagon fleet includes bikes, salon cars for light delivery and lorries, halflorry and low beds for heavy delivery.
